#  Information Security Policy

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022

**Organization:** GoldTrust Bank Ltd (Simulated Environment)

---

##  1. Purpose

The purpose of this Information Security Policy is to establish a framework for protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets at GoldTrust Bank Ltd.

This policy supports the implementation and continual improvement of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) in alignment with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

---

##  2. Scope

This policy applies to:

* All employees, contractors, and third-party users
* All information assets, systems, and networks within the ISMS scope
* All locations where organizational data is processed or stored

---

##  3. Objectives

GoldTrust Bank Ltd is committed to:

* Protecting customer and financial data from unauthorized access
* Ensuring the integrity and accuracy of information
* Maintaining availability of critical systems and services
* Complying with legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements
* Continually improving information security practices

---

## 👥 4. Roles and Responsibilities

### Management

* Ensure implementation of the ISMS
* Provide resources for information security

### IT Department

* Implement and maintain security controls
* Monitor systems and respond to incidents

### Employees

* Follow security policies and procedures
* Report security incidents promptly

---

##  5. Access Control Policy

* Access to systems shall be based on the **principle of least privilege**
* User accounts must be uniquely identifiable
* Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) should be enforced for critical systems
* Access rights must be reviewed periodically

---

##  6. Asset Management

* All information assets must be identified and classified
* Asset owners are responsible for protection and proper use
* Sensitive data must be handled securely at all times

---

##  7. Network Security

* Network access shall be controlled and monitored
* Firewalls and security controls must be implemented
* Unauthorized access to the network is prohibited

---

##  8. Cryptography

* Encryption must be used to protect sensitive data in transit and at rest
* Approved cryptographic standards must be followed

---

##  9. Secure System Development

* Secure coding practices must be adopted
* Systems should be tested for vulnerabilities before deployment
* Regular updates and patching must be enforced

---

##  10. Incident Management

* All security incidents must be reported immediately
* Incidents must be investigated and documented
* Corrective actions must be implemented

---

##  11. Backup and Recovery

* Regular backups of critical data must be performed
* Backup data must be securely stored
* Recovery procedures must be tested periodically

---

##  12. Security Awareness

* Employees must receive regular security awareness training
* Training should cover phishing, password security, and safe system usage

---

##  13. Compliance

* The organization must comply with applicable laws and regulations
* Regular audits must be conducted to ensure compliance

---

##  14. Continual Improvement

* The ISMS will be continuously monitored and improved
* Policies and controls will be reviewed regularly

---

##   15. Review and Approval

This policy shall be reviewed annually or when significant changes occur.

**Approved by:** Management

---

##  Conclusion

This Information Security Policy establishes the foundation for protecting GoldTrust Bank Ltd’s information assets and ensuring alignment with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements.
