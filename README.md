#  ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Audit & Risk Assessment

## GoldTrust Bank Ltd (Simulated Environment)

---

##  Project Overview

This project is a **comprehensive simulation of an ISO/IEC 27001:2022 audit and risk assessment** conducted for a mid-sized financial institution in Ghana, *GoldTrust Bank Ltd*.

The objective is to demonstrate practical expertise in **information security governance, risk management, and compliance (GRC)** by replicating a real-world audit engagement aligned with international standards.

This repository showcases the end-to-end process of:

* Defining audit scope
* Identifying and assessing risks
* Mapping security controls
* Conducting an internal audit
* Recommending remediation strategies

---

##  Objectives

* Perform a structured **ISO/IEC 27001:2022 risk assessment**
* Develop and maintain a **risk register and asset inventory**
* Map applicable **Annex A controls** through a Statement of Applicability (SoA)
* Simulate a **formal internal audit process**
* Identify security gaps and provide actionable recommendations

---

## Organization Profile (Simulated)

**GoldTrust Bank Ltd** is a financial services provider offering:

* Mobile banking services
* Savings and loan products
* Customer data processing systems

The organization seeks ISO/IEC 27001 certification to:

* Strengthen its security posture
* Protect sensitive financial data
* Meet regulatory and compliance requirements

##  Methodology

The project follows a structured approach aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:2022:

### 1. Scope Definition

Identification of systems and processes within the audit boundary, including:

* Core banking systems
* Mobile applications
* Internal IT infrastructure

---

### 2. Asset Identification

Creation of an asset register covering:

* Information assets (customer data)
* Physical assets (devices, servers)
* Software and network components

---

### 3. Risk Assessment

Evaluation of risks based on:

* Threats (e.g., data breaches, phishing, DDoS)
* Vulnerabilities (e.g., weak access controls)
* Likelihood and impact scoring

---

### 4. Risk Treatment

Definition of mitigation strategies such as:

* Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
* Network security controls
* Security awareness training

---

### 5. Control Mapping

Application of ISO/IEC 27001 Annex A controls via:

* Statement of Applicability (SoA)
* Justification for inclusion/exclusion

---

### 6. Audit Simulation

Execution of a simulated internal audit including:

* Audit planning
* Evidence review
* Identification of non-conformities
