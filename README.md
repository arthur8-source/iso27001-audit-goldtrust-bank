
#  ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Audit & Risk Assessment

## GoldTrust Bank Ltd (Simulated Environment)

---

##  Professional Project

This project demonstrates hands-on experience in conducting a full **ISO/IEC 27001:2022 audit**, including risk assessment, control mapping, and audit reporting aligned with industry best practices.

It simulates a real-world engagement for a financial institution and showcases practical skills in **Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)** and cybersecurity auditing.

---

##  Organization Overview

GoldTrust Bank Ltd is a simulated mid-sized financial institution providing:

* Mobile banking services
* Savings and loan products
* Customer data processing systems

---

##  Objectives

* Conduct ISO/IEC 27001 risk assessment
* Develop a structured risk register
* Map Annex A controls (Statement of Applicability)
* Perform internal audit and identify gaps
* Recommend actionable security improvements

##  Methodology

* Asset Identification
* Threat & Vulnerability Analysis
* Risk Scoring (Likelihood × Impact)
* Control Mapping (ISO Annex A)
* Internal Audit Execution

---

##  Key Findings

* Lack of Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
* Absence of centralized monitoring (SIEM)
* Weak access control mechanisms
* No formal backup and recovery strategy

---

##  Business Impact

* Reduced risk of data breaches
* Improved compliance readiness
* Strengthened access control
* Enhanced incident detection capability
