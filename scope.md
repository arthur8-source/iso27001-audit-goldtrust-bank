#  Scope of the Information Security Management System (ISMS)

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022

**Organization:** GoldTrust Bank Ltd (Simulated Environment)

---

##  Purpose

This document defines the scope of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) for GoldTrust Bank Ltd in alignment with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

The scope establishes the boundaries and applicability of the ISMS to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets.

---

##  Organizational Context

GoldTrust Bank Ltd is a mid-sized financial institution operating in Ghana, providing:

* Mobile banking services
* Savings and loan products
* Customer data processing and financial transactions

The organization relies on digital systems to deliver services and manage sensitive customer and financial data.

---

##  Scope Boundaries

###  In-Scope Systems and Processes

The ISMS covers the following:

* Core banking systems
* Mobile banking application
* Internal IT infrastructure (servers, endpoints, and network devices)
* Customer data storage and processing systems
* Employee access to information systems

---

###  Out-of-Scope Areas

The following are excluded from the ISMS scope:

* Third-party fintech integrations
* External vendor-managed systems
* Customer-owned devices outside organizational control

---

##  Assets Covered

The ISMS includes protection of:

* Information assets (customer data, financial records)
* Physical assets (servers, laptops, networking equipment)
* Software applications (banking systems, mobile apps)
* Network infrastructure

---

##  Interested Parties

The following stakeholders are considered within the ISMS:

* Customers
* Employees
* Management
* Regulatory bodies
* Third-party service providers

---

##  Regulatory and Legal Considerations

The ISMS aligns with applicable regulatory and legal requirements, including:

* Data protection and privacy laws
* Financial sector regulations in Ghana
* Industry best practices for information security

---

##  ISMS Objectives

The ISMS aims to:

* Protect sensitive financial and customer data
* Reduce cybersecurity risks and vulnerabilities
* Ensure compliance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022
* Improve incident detection and response capabilities
* Strengthen trust with customers and stakeholders

---

##  Assumptions and Dependencies

* The organization maintains basic IT infrastructure and security controls
* Management is committed to implementing ISMS improvements
* Security policies and procedures will evolve as part of continuous improvement

---

##  Scope Review

This scope will be reviewed periodically or upon significant changes to the organization’s structure, systems, or risk environment.

---

##  Conclusion

This scope defines the boundaries of the ISMS for GoldTrust Bank Ltd and provides the foundation for implementing, maintaining, and improving information security controls in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.
