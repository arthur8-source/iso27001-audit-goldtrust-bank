#  Asset Register

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022

**Organization:** GoldTrust Bank Ltd (Simulated Environment)

---

##  Purpose

The purpose of this asset register is to identify, classify, and assign ownership of information assets within the scope of the Information Security Management System (ISMS).

This ensures that all critical assets are properly managed and protected in alignment with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements.

---

##  Asset Classification Criteria

* **High:** Critical to business operations; compromise would have severe impact
* **Medium:** Important but not critical; moderate impact if compromised
* **Low:** Minimal impact on operations

##  Asset Ownership

Each asset has an assigned owner responsible for:

* Ensuring proper use and protection
* Managing access rights
* Reporting security incidents

---

##  Asset Handling Requirements

* High-value assets must be protected with strong access controls
* Sensitive data must be encrypted
* Assets must be regularly reviewed and updated

---

##  Asset Lifecycle Management

Assets must be managed throughout their lifecycle:

* **Creation/Acquisition**
* **Usage**
* **Maintenance**
* **Disposal**

---

##  Conclusion

This asset register provides a comprehensive inventory of assets within the ISMS scope and supports effective risk assessment and control implementation in alignment with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.
