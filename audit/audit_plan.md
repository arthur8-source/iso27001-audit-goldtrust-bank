#  Audit Plan

## ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Internal Audit

**Organization:** GoldTrust Bank Ltd (Simulated Environment)

---

##  1. Audit Objective

The objective of this audit is to assess the effectiveness of the Information Security Management System (ISMS) and determine the level of compliance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements.

The audit aims to:

* Evaluate implementation of security controls
* Identify gaps and non-conformities
* Provide recommendations for improvement

---

##  2. Audit Scope

The audit will cover the following areas within the ISMS:

* Core banking systems
* Mobile banking application
* Internal IT infrastructure (servers, endpoints, network devices)
* Customer data processing systems

---

##  3. Audit Criteria

The audit will be conducted against:

* ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standard (Annex A controls)
* Internal policies and procedures
* Risk assessment and risk treatment documentation

##  6. Audit Methodology

The audit will be conducted using the following methods:

* **Document Review:** Policies, procedures, risk registers, and SoA
* **Interviews:** Discussions with key personnel (simulated)
* **Observation:** Review of system configurations and practices
* **Sampling:** Selection of representative controls for testing

---

##  7. Audit Evidence

Evidence to be reviewed includes:

* Risk assessment documentation
* Statement of Applicability (SoA)
* Access control records
* System configurations
* Security policies and procedures

---

##  8. Risk-Based Approach

The audit will prioritize areas with higher risk, including:

* Authentication and access control
* Data protection and encryption
* Network security
* Monitoring and incident detection

---

##  9. Reporting

The audit findings will be documented in an audit report including:

* Identified non-conformities
* Risk levels (High, Medium, Low)
* Recommendations for remediation

---

##  10. Follow-Up

Follow-up actions will include:

* Review of corrective actions
* Verification of implemented controls
* Continuous improvement of the ISMS

---

##  Conclusion

This audit plan provides a structured approach to evaluating the ISMS of GoldTrust Bank Ltd and ensures alignment with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standards.

The outcome of this audit will support the organization in strengthening its information security posture and achieving compliance.
